How to Use Construct Ease

Follow these simple steps — no technical knowledge needed!

1

Pick Your Role

Are you a House Owner, Architect, Builder, or looking for Interior Design? Click your role on the home page to get started.

2

Estimate Your Cost

Enter your plot size and quality preference. Our system instantly shows you the total cost, broken down into materials, labor, and extras — no hidden charges!

3

Choose Your Home

Browse 1BHK, 2BHK, 3BHK, and 4BHK designs with real images, expected costs, and room details. Click "View Details" to explore any option.

4

Use the AI Hub

Visit the AI Command Center for smarter decisions — scan contracts for risks, predict your timeline, get architecture recommendations, and track material prices.

5

Design Your Interior

Go to Interior Studio to estimate furniture & décor costs, browse budget-friendly showrooms, and find top interior designers near you — all with live website links!

6

Connect & Build

Find verified architects, builders and contractors through our marketplace. View their work, check ratings, and hire — all from one place.
